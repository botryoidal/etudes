Chapter · 04

Five principles
for building
with care.

P01

Begin with the end in mind.

Every interaction has a destination. Design the arrival first, then trace the path backward. The user should never wonder what comes next.

P02

Motion is meaning.

Animation is not decoration. It is language. Each transition should communicate a state change, spatial relationship, or hierarchy of importance.

P03

Restraint is a feature.

What you omit matters more than what you include. Negative space, silence, and pause are tools as important as color and type.

P04

Type carries the tone.

Typography is 95% of design. Before color, before layout, before motion — the voice of the work lives in the letterforms.

P05

Ship with intention.

Every pixel is a choice. Every delay is a promise. Every detail reveals whether you cared enough to notice.
